{\descr{Знайти Інтеграл}}

$$
\int \dfrac{\d{x}}{1+cos2x} = \Bigg |
  \begin{array}{r l }
    \cos^2x = & \dfrac{1+cos2x}{2} \\
    2 \cos^2x = & 1+cos2x \\
  \end{array}
\Bigg |
$$

$$
  \int \dfrac{\d{x}}{2\cos^2x}
  = \dfrac{1}{2} \int \dfrac{\d{x}}{\cos^2x}
  = \dfrac{1}{2} \cdot \tg{x} + C
  = \frac{tg{x}}{2} + C.
$$

$$
  \boxed{\frac{tg{x}}{2} + C}
$$
