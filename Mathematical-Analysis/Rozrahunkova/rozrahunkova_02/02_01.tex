{\descr{Обчислити визначений інтеграл}}

$$
  \int^e_1 \dfrac{\ln^5x}{x}\d{x}
= \int^e_1 \ln^5x \dfrac{1}{x}\d{x}
=
\Bigg |
  \begin{array}{rl rl r r}
    t =& \ln{x}               & x = & e^t &x_2 =e & t_2 = 1 \\
    \d{t} = & (\ln{x})'\d{x}  & \d{t} = & \dfrac{1}{x}\d{x}&x_1 =1 & t_1 = 0 \\
  \end{array}
\Bigg |
= \int^1_0 t^5\d{t}
$$

$$
= \int^1_0 t^5\d{t}
= \dfrac{t^6}{6} \Bigg |^1_0
= \dfrac{1}{6} - \dfrac{0}{6}
= \boxed{\dfrac{1}{6}}
$$
