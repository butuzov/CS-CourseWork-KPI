4) \sum_{n=0}^\infty \dfrac{2^n}{n^{10}}
  \Rightarrow
  \lim_{n \to \infty} \dfrac{2^{n+1}}{(n+1)^{10}} \times \dfrac{n^{10}}{2^n}
=  \lim_{n \to \infty} \dfrac{2n^{10} }{(n+1)^{10} }
=  2 \lim_{n \to \infty} \Big( \dfrac{n}{n+1} \Big)^{10}
= 2
$$
