{\descr{Знайти Інтеграл}}

$$
  \int \tg^2{\dfrac{x}{2}}\d{x} = \Bigg |
    \begin{array}{l c  l}
      t = \dfrac{x}{2} & & \d{x} = (x)'\d{t}  \\
      x = 2t & & \d{x} = 2\d{t}
    \end{array}
  \Bigg |
  = \int \tg^2{t} \cdot 2\d{t}
  = 2 \int \tg^2{t}\d{t}
  = 2\tg{t}-2t+C
  = 2\tg{\dfrac{x}{2}} - x + C
$$

$$
  \boxed{2\tg{\dfrac{x}{2}} - x + C}
$$
